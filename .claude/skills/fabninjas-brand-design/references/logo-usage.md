# FabNinjas Logo Usage

**Source:** `FabNinjas_BrandGuidelines.pdf`, page 5 — *"Logo."*

Verbatim rules from the source:
- *"Our logo is a hand-lettered one unit for the whole name."*
- *"The complete art needs to be used as is and in no way should be changed anywhere apart from the colour of it."*
- *"There is a favicon version of the same that can be used in cases where the visibility of the whole logo is hampered due to limited space."*

## What the logo is

- **Primary logo:** hand-lettered `fab` script with `NINJAS` (letter-spaced, bold)
  locked beneath it. It is **artwork, not type** — treat it as one indivisible unit.
- **Favicon:** an `fn` monogram, for small or tight spaces only.

## Colour: the only permitted change

The source allows changing **only the colour** of the logo. Approved logo colours:
- **ink `#282643`** (default), **white** (reversed), or **solid black** (mono print).
- Do **not** colour the logo orange, purple, mauve, cream, gradients, or any off-brand hue.

Pick the version with enough contrast against its background (see `colour-system.md`):
navy logo on light grounds, white logo on ink/purple/dark grounds.

## Full logo vs favicon

- Use the **full logo** by default.
- Switch to the **favicon** only when space is limited or the full logo would render too
  small to read (favicons, app icons, avatars, tiny UI corners).

## Prohibited treatments (from "used as is … in no way changed")

- Don't redraw, re-typeset, or recreate the hand-lettering — always place the supplied art.
- Don't stretch, condense, skew, rotate, or otherwise distort it.
- Don't recolour it outside ink / white / black.
- Don't add effects (shadows, glows, gradients, strokes, textures).
- Don't rearrange the lockup (e.g. `NINJAS` beside `fab`) or separate the two words.
- Don't place the navy/black logo on a dark background, or the white logo on a light one.
- Don't set it on a low-contrast or busy area of a photo — use a solid panel or the reversed version.

## Clear space & minimum size — NOT specified in source (recommendation)

The guidelines give **no** clear-space or minimum-size values. Until brand confirms,
use these conservative defaults and flag them as unofficial:

- **Clear space:** keep padding around the logo equal to the height of the `N` in `NINJAS`
  (roughly the favicon's height) clear of other elements on all sides.
- **Minimum size:** full logo no smaller than **120px** wide on screen (≈ 25mm print);
  below that, switch to the favicon. Favicon no smaller than **24px**.

Replace these with official values if/when the brand provides them.
